Please Confirm Cancellation
Driver is allowed to charge a deposit of 20% or less of total fare to the passenger ahead of trip. If passenger cancels or does not show up prior to the trip, then the driver is entitled to keep the deposit amount as compensation for lost revenue. If driver cancels or does not show up prior to the trip, then the rider is entitled to get the deposit back. Drivers who do this 3 times will be permanently removed from the platform.
